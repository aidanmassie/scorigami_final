WHAT IS SCORIGAMI?
Scorigami is a concept created by Jon Bois of SB Nation. Scorigami is the art of creating final scores that have never happened before in NFL history. Due to the unique nature of scoring in the NFL, such as 2 points and 8 points, as well as an unhumanly act of scoring a single point in a game, there are hundreds of different scores possible that have never happened.

NFL Scorigami affects the game of football in no way, shape or form. Scorigami is simply a fun, statistical anomaly for statheads, like myself, to enjoy and browse for hours on end. For more infomation on Scorigami, watch the video below created by the founder of Scorigami, Jon Bois.
